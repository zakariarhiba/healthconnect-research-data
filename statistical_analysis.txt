STATISTICAL SIGNIFICANCE TESTING
########################################

Hypothesis Test: One-Way ANOVA

Null Hypothesis (H0): All architectures have equal mean latency
Alternative Hypothesis (H1): At least one architecture differs significantly

ANOVA Results:
F-Statistic: F(3, 99,996) = 45,230.5
P-Value: < 0.0001 (highly significant)
Conclusion: REJECT NULL HYPOTHESIS

The differences in latency between architectures are HIGHLY STATISTICALLY 
SIGNIFICANT (p < 0.001). This is not due to chance but represents real,
fundamental differences in architecture performance.
